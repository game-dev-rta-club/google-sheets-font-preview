# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want Font Preview Sidebar to be a welcoming project for people with different backgrounds, languages, experience levels, and perspectives.

Contributors and maintainers are expected to participate with respect, patience, and care.

## Expected behavior

- Be kind and constructive.
- Assume good intent, especially across language and cultural differences.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.
- Respect differing levels of experience.
- Help make discussions useful for future readers.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Trolling, repeated disruption, or bad-faith participation.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or block participants when behavior harms the project or its community.

If you see behavior that violates this code of conduct, please contact the maintainers through GitHub.

## Attribution

This code of conduct is inspired by the Contributor Covenant and adapted to keep this project lightweight and easy to maintain.
